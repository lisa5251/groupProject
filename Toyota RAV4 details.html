Toyota RAV4

The Toyota RAV4 combines rugged capabilities with refined comfort, making it one of the best compact SUVs in its class.

Toyota RAV4 - Versatility Meets Adventure

The Toyota RAV4 is designed for those who crave adventure without compromising on comfort. It blends efficient fuel economy with an adventurous spirit, making it perfect for families and outdoor enthusiasts alike.

Key Specifications

Feature Details
Engine 2.5L 4-Cylinder
Horsepower 203 HP
0-60 mph 5.9 - 7.8 seconds
Top Speed 120 mph
Price Starts at $28,000

Why Buy a Toyota RAV4?

The Toyota RAV4 is a great choice for anyone seeking a reliable, fuel-efficient, and adventure-ready SUV. Its spacious interior and excellent handling make it perfect for both city driving and weekend getaways.
