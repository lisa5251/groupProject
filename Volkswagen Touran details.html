Volkswagen Touran

The Volkswagen Touran is a spacious and practical MPV, offering versatility, comfort, and advanced technology for families and long-distance travelers.

Volkswagen Touran - Versatile and Efficient

The Touran is designed for modern families, providing ample space, safety features, and an efficient engine lineup that ensures a smooth ride.

Key Specifications

Feature Details
Engine 1.5L TSI / 2.0L TDI
Horsepower 150 - 190 HP
0-60 mph 8.9 - 10.2 seconds

Price Starts at $30,000

Why Buy a Volkswagen Touran?

The Volkswagen Touran is a perfect choice for those seeking a reliable and spacious MPV. With excellent safety features, fuel efficiency, and comfortable seating for up to 7 passengers, it stands out as a practical and family-friendly vehicle.
